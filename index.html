Our Services

Insight PBS does one thing – Behaviour Support! We deliver the many important facets of Behaviour Support:

Positive Behaviour Support

Our PBS practitioners conduct comprehensive, evidence-based assessments to develop individualized support plans.

Specialist Support Coordination

We enable clients to identify and reach their personal goals through specialized support coordination.

Supported Independent Living

We provide comprehensive support to clients to help them live independently and make informed decisions about their lives.

Functional Behaviour Assessment

Our practitioners follow the Insight PBS Process to develop a thorough understanding of behaviour and underlying causes.

Development of PBS Strategies

Evidence has shown that positive and proactive strategies do more to promote higher levels of quality of life in all people involved.

Behaviour Support Plans

Insight PBS develops interim and comprehensive Behaviour Support Plans to support proactive and positive strategies.
